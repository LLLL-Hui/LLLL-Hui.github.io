    • Research interests

    My current focus of research is in learning from noisy distribution, particularly I tries to introduce label-noise learning to Space Situation Awareness.

    1. Label-noise learning. Utilizing instance with noisy labels to train networks.
    2. Space debris detection, vital in Space Situation Awareness, requiring detection small and dim debris from observation. Noisy labels widely exist, thus label-noise learning is adopted
